User supplied data
Any content that was directly submitted by you is considered public. All such content must non-licensed and related to StarCraft2.
3rd party connections
Such data will be stored in the DB according to the Privacy Policy of the corresponding service. You can remove your data at any time by unlinking your accounts.
Revealed/Unmasked accounts
Revealed accounts won't be removed. We usually remove some info if users request it, but this is a special case because account removal will trigger a chain reaction from other users and the whole feature will be useless. Account revealing is based on public data and will not be removed.
Data removal
We strictly follow the Blizzard ToS, which forces us to permanently delete the data within 30 days after it was deleted from the Blizzard's API. Bear in mind that StartCraft®2 leaderboards are deemed as public data(section 15). You can remove some of your data by submitting a data removal request to Blizzard, disabling 3rd party access in settings of your Blizzard account, or using the BattleNet data manager.
